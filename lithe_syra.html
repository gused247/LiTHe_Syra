LiTHe Syra Student Run Club
NYC energy · Linköping legs · orange signal

Run the campus. Own the pace.

LiTHe Syra is a student running club at Linköping University, founded in 2007 for anyone who loves to train together – regardless of pace or ambition.
Whether you're aiming for a personal best or simply enjoy running with others, you'll feel right at home.

We train both indoors at Campushallen and outdoors several times a week, throughout almost the entire academic year. LiTHe Syra works closely with LiTHe Vilse (orienteering) and LiU Elitidrott (campus sports). All our sessions are gathered in a shared training calendar, where you can also sign up. In that calendar, LiTHe Syra's workouts are marked in orange, LiTHe Vilse's in purple, and LiU Elitidrott's in blue.
